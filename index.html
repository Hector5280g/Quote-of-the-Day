Great influencers of our time

Gary Vee

Gary Vee is an entrepreneur, he thrives on being proactive and not making excuses for your problems. Gary worked at his family's liquor store from the ages of 19 to 30. He would put in 60-80 hour work weeks.Gary found different ways of making passive income. While working he saved and invested his money. Big opportunities came while building his capital such as being able to invest in facebook, Twitter, Tumblr, Venmo, Snapchat, and Coinbase. The companies are all high tech companies that helped his portfolio explode. He wasn’t always lucky on his investments. Uber offered him early investment 3 times, but he denied it everytime, because he didn’t believe in it. But it turns out that Uber now is one of the biggest companies till this day. Gary Vee now helps preach the message of “ Don’t let anyone tell you what you can and cannot do, but nothing comes easy, you have to be ready to put the work in when it matters.”

Mark Cuban

Mark Cuban (born July 31, 1958) is an American billionaire entrepreneur, television personality, and media proprietor whose net worth is an estimated $4.8 billion, according to Forbes, and ranked No. 177 on the 2020 Forbes 400 list. He is the owner of the Dallas Mavericks professional basketball team of the National Basketball Association (NBA) and the co-owner of 2929 Entertainment. He is also one of the main "shark" investors on the ABC reality television series Shark Tank. Elon Musk

Elon Reeve Musk FRS (born June 28, 1971) is a business magnate and investor. He is the founder, CEO and chief engineer of SpaceX; angel investor, CEO and product architect of Tesla, Inc.; founder of The Boring Company; co-founder of Neuralink and OpenAI; president of Musk Foundation; and owner of Twitter, Inc. With an estimated net worth of around $210 billion as of October 27, 2022, Musk is the wealthiest person in the world according to both the Bloomberg Billionaires Index and Forbes's real-time billionaires list.
